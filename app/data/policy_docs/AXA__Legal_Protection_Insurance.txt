Section 1: Coverage
Covers legal costs for disputes in private and professional life, including lawyer fees, court costs, and mediation.
Section 2: Exclusions
Excludes pre-existing disputes, criminal cases, and intentional violations.
Section 3: Claims
Legal assistance must be requested before engaging a lawyer.
Section 4: Coverage Limit
Up to 300,000 EUR per case.
Section 5: Waiting Period
Three-month waiting period applies for most disputes.
Section 6: Termination
Policy can be terminated annually with one month's notice.
