Section 1: Insured Items
Covers furniture, electronics, clothing, and valuables against fire, theft, and water damage.
Section 2: Coverage Limits
Maximum coverage is 50,000 EUR. Jewelry and valuables are covered up to 5,000 EUR.
Section 3: Exclusions
Flood damage and wear-and-tear are not covered.
Section 4: Claims
Claims must be filed within 14 days of discovery. Police report required for theft.
Section 5: Premiums
Annual premium is based on property value and location.
Section 6: Policy Changes
Policyholder must notify insurer of major purchases or renovations.
