Section 1: Insured Property
Covers residential buildings, outbuildings, and garages.
Section 2: Covered Risks
Fire, storm, hail, and certain types of water damage.
Section 3: Exclusions
Earthquake and flood coverage require additional riders.
Section 4: Claims
Claims must be reported within 7 days. Emergency repairs are reimbursed.
Section 5: Coverage Limit
Based on reconstruction value of the property.
Section 6: Premiums
Premiums depend on location, building age, and construction type.
