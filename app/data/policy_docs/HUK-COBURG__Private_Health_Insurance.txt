Section 1: Coverage
Reimburses medical expenses for outpatient, inpatient, and specialist treatments.
Section 2: Dental and Alternative Medicine
Includes dental coverage and alternative medicine up to policy limits.
Section 3: Deductible
500 EUR annual deductible applies.
Section 4: Exclusions
Cosmetic procedures and experimental treatments are excluded.
Section 5: Claims
Claims must be submitted with original invoices.
Section 6: Premiums
Premiums based on age, health status, and coverage level.
