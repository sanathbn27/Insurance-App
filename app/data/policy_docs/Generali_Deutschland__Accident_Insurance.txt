Section 1: Coverage
Financial compensation in case of accidental injury resulting in disability or death.
Section 2: Worldwide Coverage
Covers accidents worldwide, 24/7.
Section 3: Exclusions
Professional sports and intentional acts are excluded.
Section 4: Claims
Claims must be filed within 30 days. Medical documentation required.
Section 5: Coverage Limit
Up to 500,000 EUR for disability, 100,000 EUR for death.
Section 6: Premiums
Premiums based on occupation and risk level.
