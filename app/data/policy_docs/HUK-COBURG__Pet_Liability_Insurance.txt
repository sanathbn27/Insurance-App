Section 1: Coverage
Covers damages caused by your pet to third parties, including property and injury.
Section 2: Covered Animals
Includes dogs and cats. Excludes exotic animals.
Section 3: Exclusions
Intentional acts and damages to family members are excluded.
Section 4: Claims
Claims must be reported within 14 days.
Section 5: Coverage Limit
Up to 2 million EUR per incident.
Section 6: Premiums
Premiums depend on animal type and breed.
