Section 1: Medical Coverage
Emergency medical expenses abroad, including hospitalization and repatriation.
Section 2: Trip Cancellation
Reimburses non-refundable costs if trip is cancelled for covered reasons.
Section 3: Lost Luggage
Lost luggage is covered up to 1,000 EUR.
Section 4: Exclusions
Pre-existing medical conditions and high-risk activities are excluded.
Section 5: Claims
Claims must be submitted within 30 days of return.
Section 6: Assistance
24/7 emergency hotline available worldwide.
