Section 1: Coverage
This policy covers personal liability for damages caused to third parties, including accidental property damage and bodily injury.
Section 2: Exclusions
Exclusions apply to intentional acts, damages caused by motor vehicles, and professional activities.
Section 3: Claims Process
Claims must be reported within 30 days of the incident. Supporting documentation is required.
Section 4: Limits
Maximum coverage is 10 million EUR per incident. Legal costs are included up to 100,000 EUR.
Section 5: Additional Benefits
Includes coverage for loss of keys and volunteer activities.
Section 6: Termination
Policy may be terminated with 3 months' notice before renewal.
