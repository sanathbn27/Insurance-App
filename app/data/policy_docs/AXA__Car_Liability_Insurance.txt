Section 1: Coverage
Provides mandatory liability coverage for damages caused to others while operating your vehicle.
Section 2: Exclusions
Does not cover damage to your own car, intentional acts, or racing.
Section 3: Coverage Limit
100 million EUR for property damage, 15 million EUR for bodily injury per person.
Section 4: Claims
Accidents must be reported within 48 hours. Police report required for major incidents.
Section 5: Additional Services
Includes roadside assistance and legal support.
Section 6: Premiums
Premiums depend on driver history and vehicle type.
