Section 1: Coverage
Covers theft and damage to bicycles, including vandalism and accidents.
Section 2: Roadside Assistance
Roadside assistance within Germany.
Section 3: E-bikes
E-bikes are covered up to 3,000 EUR.
Section 4: Exclusions
Wear and tear, and racing are not covered.
Section 5: Claims
Claims must be reported within 7 days. Police report required for theft.
Section 6: Premiums
Premiums depend on bike value and location.
